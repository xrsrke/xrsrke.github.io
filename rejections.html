Rejections

In high school, a teacher told him he would never get anywhere in life. No one would ever hire him for a job. He was ranked the last student in his class. He was almost kicked out twice.

During his last two years in high school, he ran a software startup. He was rejected 10 out of 12 times when pitching. He reached out to more than 10 investors and none of them accepted. He went to the office of one investor three times but couldn't get an appointment. He deliberately calculated when exactly one investor was going to be at a specific location and planned to wait there 24/7 for 3 days straight, without leaving, until he could meet them. He met that investor at 0:30 am at the airport's exit door.

He tried to learn a deep learning course three times before he finally understood it.
